Manufacturing Excellence

Why the World's Best Drones Start at Yomura

Agricultural drones operate in the most punishing environments — rain, pesticides, UV exposure, impacts, and extreme temperature swings. Yomura's advanced injection molding technology delivers the structural integrity and material resilience these conditions demand.

Two-Shot / Over-Molding Mastery
Multi-material components molded in a single cycle — creating IP68-rated waterproof housings that bond hard structural shells with soft-touch sealing surfaces. No secondary assembly needed.
Weather & Chemical Resistance
Engineered polymer formulations resist pesticide corrosion, UV degradation, and temperature extremes from –10 °C to 60 °C — exceeding the service life of conventional drone materials by 3×.
Precision Structural Design
CNC/EDM tooling achieves ±0.02 mm tolerances on drone frame components. Every motor mount, arm connector, and landing gear bracket is engineered for vibration resistance and crashworthiness.
Lightweight Material Science
Advanced PC/ABS blends and glass-filled nylons deliver best-in-class strength-to-weight ratios — critical for maximizing flight time, payload capacity, and operational range per battery charge.
From Concept to Field

How We Build Together

Our co-engineering model integrates Earthgen's aerospace R&D with Yomura's manufacturing infrastructure — from the first CAD sketch to final field deployment.

01
Co-Design & DFM
Earthgen's drone engineers and Yomura's mold designers collaborate on Design for Manufacturability — optimizing form, fit, and function from day one.
02
Rapid Prototyping
3D-printed prototypes validated in Yomura's polymer test lab. Material selection, wall thickness, and stress analysis completed before tooling investment.
03
Precision Tooling & Molding
In-house CNC/EDM fabrication of production molds. Two-shot injection and over-molding processes produce finished, sealed components in a single cycle.
04
Assembly & Delivery
Yomura supplies Earthgen with precision-molded parts, integrating avionics and systems through rigorous quality audits and seamless global logistics fulfillment.
